📐 TVI Formula

TVI = 0.40 × Traffic Density + 0.30 × Alt. Route Factor + 0.20 × Road Width Factor + 0.10 × Critical Importance

Alt. Route Factor

1 route→100, 2→50, 3→33...

Road Width Factor

2 lanes→80, 3→50, 4→30

Critical Importance

Yes→100, No→0

Risk Thresholds

🔴 High Risk  →  TVI ≥ 70
🟡 Medium Risk →  TVI ≥ 40
🟢 Low Risk   →  TVI < 40
